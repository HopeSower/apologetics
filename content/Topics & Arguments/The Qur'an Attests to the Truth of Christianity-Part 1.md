## 💡 Key Points & Apologetic Arguments

### Islam Claims All Prophets Were Muslims

- According to Islam, "Muslim" means one who submits to God’s will.
- Islam teaches that Adam, Abraham, Isaac, Jacob, Moses, and even Jesus were Muslims, submitting to Allah, the one true God [[Quran 22_78]].
- The Quran explicitly claims the religion of Abraham was Islam, not Judaism or Christianity [[Quran 22_78]].
### Jesus and His Disciples as Muslims According to the Quran

- The Quran claims Jesus and His disciples (like Peter, James, John) were Muslims, followers who submitted exclusively to Allah.
- The disciples explicitly declare themselves Muslims in the Quran (Quran 3:52-53, 5:111), not Christians.
