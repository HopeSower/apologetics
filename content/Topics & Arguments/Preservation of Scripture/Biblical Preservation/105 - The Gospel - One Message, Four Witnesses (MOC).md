# The Gospel: One Message, Four Witnesses

tags: #gospels #bible #apologetics/gospels #moc

The four Gospels are a profound strength of the biblical testimony.

1.  **The Meaning of the Word:** The "Gospel" is the message itself. -> [[106 - Euangelion - The Good News]]
2.  **The Meaning of the Titles:** The titles point to one message through multiple witnesses. -> [[107 - Kata - According To]]
3.  **The Legal Principle:** This fulfills God's own standard for establishing truth. -> [[108 - The Principle of Multiple Witnesses]]
4.  **The Four Perspectives:** Each Gospel author wrote for a specific audience, which explains their unique focus.
