# Manuscript Evidence for the New Testament

tags: #textual_criticism #evidence #manuscripts

The evidence for the NT is an "embarrassment of riches."

### Manuscript Count Comparison:
| Work              | Author  | Date Written | Earliest Copy | Time Gap       | No. of MSS         |
| :---------------- | :------ | :----------- | :------------ | :------------- | :----------------- |
| **New Testament** | Various | 40-100 AD    | ~130 AD       | **~30-50 yrs** | **5,800+** (Greek) |
| Iliad             | Homer   | 800 BC       | ~400 BC       | ~400 yrs       | ~1,900             |
| Gallic Wars       | Caesar  | 50 BC        | ~900 AD       | ~950 yrs       | ~250               |
| Histories         | Plato   | 400 BC       | ~900 AD       | ~1,200 yrs     | ~200               |

### Key Takeaway:
We have **more manuscripts** and **earlier manuscripts** for the New Testament than for any other book from the ancient world. This allows scholars to cross-reference and reconstruct the original text with incredible accuracy.
