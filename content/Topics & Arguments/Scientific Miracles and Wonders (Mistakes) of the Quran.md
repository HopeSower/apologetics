### What classifies as a divine miracle?

A statement counts as a divine miracle **only if it satisfies all three conditions simultaneously**:

- **NEW knowledge** – no person or culture possessed the information at the time it was uttered.
- **Beyond guesswork or observation** – the fact could not be reached by trial‑and‑error, keen eyesight, or the best science/ philosophy then available.
- **Absolute clarity** – the wording must state the datum plainly enough to be recognized _before_ outside explanations are imported.
