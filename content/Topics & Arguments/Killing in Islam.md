## 1. The Traditional Sunni View on Apostasy

Classical Sunni teachings consistently affirm that apostasy is a capital offense. The original texts leave no doubt about the severe consequences for those who abandon the faith.

---

## 2. The Traditional Shia Position on Apostasy

Traditional Shia scholarship is equally clear: apostasy, especially when committed by a born-Muslim, is met with the death penalty. This steadfast view is rooted in longstanding doctrinal interpretations.

---

## 3. Lethal Force in the Context of Jihad

Both Sunni and Shia sources set forth explicit guidelines for the use of lethal force in warfare. These measures are traditionally understood as necessary for defending the Muslim community and preserving the integrity of the faith.
